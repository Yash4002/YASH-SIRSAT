How I Work

Design Process

A research-backed flow that keeps every decision tied to a real user problem, from first discovery to final delivery.

01 — DISCOVER

Discover

Understanding the business goal, the user, and the constraints before touching a single pixel.

02 — RESEARCH

Research

Competitor analysis, user interviews, and data to ground every decision in evidence.

03 — DEFINE

Define

Translating research into personas, journey maps, and a clear problem statement.

04 — IDEATE

Ideate

Exploring multiple directions through sketches and information architecture.

05 — WIREFRAME

Wireframe

Structuring layout and flow before any visual styling begins.

06 — PROTOTYPE

Prototype

Building interactive, high-fidelity prototypes that feel like the real product.

07 — TEST

Test

Usability testing to validate decisions and surface friction early.

08 — DELIVER

Deliver

Polished, developer-ready handoff with a documented design system.
